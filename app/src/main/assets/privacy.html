Privacy Policy short-form notice
We respect your privacy. This privacy policy sets out the manner in which we collect, use, disclose and manage your personal information.

Why we collect information
Reverd App transmits minimal information to support internal operations of the service such as user authentication. Reverd.com website may collect information voluntarily provided by members to maintain user profiles and to keep records in the Reverd Phone Book accurate and up-to-date.

What Information is collected 
Reverd App does not need personal information in order to operate. Reverd App generates and transmits to our server a persistent identifier which is a string of alphanumeric characters. It refers to the installed app instance only. Phone number could be part of the identifier for some platforms. Our persistent identifiers cannot be used to correlate user data and are in compliance with the Limited Exceptions to COPPA. Reverd.com website collects telephone and fax numbers, mobile phone numbers, names, postal address, e-mail address and digital images.

How we collect information
Reverd App automatically generates and transmits persistent identifier at time of installation. Reverd App does not collect personal information. Personally identifiable information may be collected via web form at reverd.com website when registered users complete or update their profile voluntarily.

Use of information
Reverd uses the information for user authentication and to support internal operations of the service. Some of user provided information may be used for communication purposes and to keep the records in the Reverd Phone Book up-to-date.

Sharing of information
Reverd will not share your e-mail address with third parties except as authorized by you or required by applicable law. Reverd’s Phone Book is publicly available on the Internet.

Security of personal information
Reverd follows best practices implementing appropriate technology and procedures to secure and safeguard your personal information. Passwords are secured while in-transit and encrypted on the server.

Despite of our diligent efforts no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure due to current technology.

Correcting or updating Information 
Registered users can change, correct or update their information at any time by logging to their account.

Opting-out and information deletion
Reverd App can be uninstalled at any time. All information residing on your phone will be deleted when you uninstall the Reverd App.

Your consent
By using Reverd App, you consent to Reverd Privacy Policy and to receive updates, alerts or reminders. In an unlikely event you consent to indemnify Reverd for any loss or damage deemed to result from using Reverd App.

Changes in privacy policy
Reverd may change or revise this Privacy Policy as necessary by posting such revision on its website at https://reverd.com/legal/privacy-policy

Contact us
If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy please feel free to contact us.
