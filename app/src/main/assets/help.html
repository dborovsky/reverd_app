Getting started
Go to settings and select your language from the language menu.

More options in settings
Sync with e-mail is an optional but recommended feature that synchronizes your app with a free membership account in Reverd.com.

Block calls with no caller ID feature is ON by default. Turn it off if you want to receive calls with no caller ID from numbers that are not in the blacklist.

You can block calls from certain area codes or from whole countries by selecting them.

Add a phone number to blacklist manually
Tap the + icon.
Fill in the form and tap the Add button.
Report the number to the community blacklist (recommended).

Move phone numbers between your lists
Tap the edit icon.
Select phone numbers to be moved.
Tap the list icon to move the numbers instantly.

If you want to receive calls from a number in the blacklist just move it to the whitelist or vice versa.

NOTE: Moving numbers between lists affects only records in your phone. This feature does not change the phone number status in the community blacklist.

Automatic updates for better protection (recommended)
Although our free application is fully functional we recommend that you subscribe for our updates of the community blacklist. Our updates contain the latest reported harassing phone numbers providing you with better protection against newest spam.
